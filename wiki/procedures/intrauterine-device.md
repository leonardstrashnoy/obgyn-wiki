# Intrauterine Device

An IUD is a highly effective long-acting reversible contraceptive (LARC) placed in the uterine cavity.

## Types

- **Copper IUD**: Non-hormonal; spermicidal; 10-12 year duration
- **Levonorgestrel IUD**: Progestin-containing; 3-8 year duration; reduces bleeding

## Insertion

Office procedure with speculum, tenaculum, uterine sound, IUD inserter.

## Evidence Level

1A
