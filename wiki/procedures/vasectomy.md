# Vasectomy

Male sterilization via bilateral occlusion of the vas deferens.

## Procedure

Office or outpatient procedure under local anesthesia. Vas deferens accessed through small scrotal incision or no-scalpel technique.

## Effectiveness

99.9% (most effective contraceptive method)

## Follow-up

Required semen analysis at 8-12 weeks and 20 ejaculations to confirm azoospermia.

## Reversal

Microsurgical vasovasostomy has 40-90% patency rates; pregnancy rates vary by interval.

## Evidence Level

1A
