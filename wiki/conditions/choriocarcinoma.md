## Clinical Definition

Choriocarcinoma is a highly malignant trophoblastic tumor characterized by hematogenous metastasis to lung, brain, liver, and vagina. It may follow any gestational event (mole, abortion, term pregnancy, ectopic) (2B).

## Management

- **Low-risk:** Single-agent methotrexate or actinomycin-D
- **High-risk (FIGO >6, metastasis to brain/liver):** EMA-CO (etoposide, methotrexate, actinomycin-D, cyclophosphamide, vincristine / Oncovin)
- Cure rate > 90% even with metastases
