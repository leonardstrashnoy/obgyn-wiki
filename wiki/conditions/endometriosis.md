## Clinical Definition

Endometriosis is the presence of endometrial-like tissue outside the uterine cavity, causing inflammation, pain, and adhesions. It affects ~10% of reproductive-age women and is associated with infertility (2B).

## Pregnancy Considerations

- Most women conceive without issues
- Increased risk of ectopic pregnancy
- Rare decidualization of endometriotic lesions in pregnancy
- Does not worsen with most pregnancies
