# Hypothyroidism

Hypothyroidism is a common endocrine disorder that can cause menstrual irregularities, including amenorrhea and heavy menstrual bleeding.

## Clinical Relevance

Thyroid dysfunction affects 5-10% of women and is a reversible cause of menstrual disorders. TSH screening indicated in amenorrhea workup.
