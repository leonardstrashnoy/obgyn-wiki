## Clinical Definition

TTTS is a complication of monochorionic twin pregnancies caused by unbalanced placental vascular anastomoses, leading to polyhydramnios in the recipient and oligohydramnios in the donor (1B).

## Staging (Quintero)

- I: Poly/oligo sequence
- II: Donor bladder not visible
- III: Doppler abnormalities
- IV: Hydrops
- V: Demise of one or both

## Management

- **Laser photocoagulation** of placental anastomoses: Standard for Stage II–IV (1B — Eurofetus trial)
- **Amnioreduction:** Palliative if laser unavailable
- **Selective reduction:** For severe cases with one nonviable twin
