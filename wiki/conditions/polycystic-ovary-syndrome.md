# Polycystic Ovary Syndrome

PCOS is the most common endocrine disorder of reproductive-aged women and a leading cause of anovulatory infertility.

## Clinical Relevance

Diagnosis requires 2/3: oligo/anovulation, hyperandrogenism, polycystic ovarian morphology.
