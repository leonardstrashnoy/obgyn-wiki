# Cardiovascular Disease

Cardiovascular disease (CVD) is the leading cause of death in women, with risk increasing after menopause.

## Clinical Relevance

Premenopausal women have lower CVD risk than men; postmenopause risk equalizes. Timing hypothesis for MHT.
