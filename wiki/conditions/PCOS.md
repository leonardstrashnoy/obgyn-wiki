## Clinical Definition

PCOS is a heterogeneous endocrine disorder defined by hyperandrogenism, ovulatory dysfunction, and polycystic ovarian morphology (Rotterdam criteria). It is the most common cause of anovulatory infertility (1B).

## Pregnancy Complications

- Gestational diabetes (40–50%)
- Preeclampsia
- Preterm birth
- Pregnancy loss (early)
- Fetal macrosomia

## Management in Pregnancy

- Early GDM screening
- Low-dose aspirin for preeclampsia prevention
- Metformin may reduce GDM risk (contested) (1B)
