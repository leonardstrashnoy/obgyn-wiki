# Sexually Transmitted Infections

STIs include chlamydia, gonorrhea, syphilis, HIV, HPV, HSV, and trichomoniasis. Prevention through barrier methods and vaccination (HPV).

## Clinical Relevance

Major public health concern with reproductive complications including PID, infertility, and cervical cancer.
