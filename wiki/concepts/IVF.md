## Clinical Definition

IVF is an assisted reproductive technology involving ovarian stimulation, oocyte retrieval, fertilization in vitro, and embryo transfer. It is the most effective treatment for most infertility etiologies (1B).

## Key Risks

- Multiple pregnancy (20–30% if multiple embryos transferred)
- Ovarian hyperstimulation syndrome (OHSS)
- Ectopic pregnancy (2–5%)
- Preterm birth / low birth weight
