# Hormone Replacement Therapy

Systemic administration of estrogen (with or without progestogen) for treatment of menopausal symptoms or prevention of osteoporosis.

## Clinical Relevance

First-line therapy for vasomotor symptoms; benefits and risks must be individualized.
