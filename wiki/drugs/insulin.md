## Clinical Definition

Insulin is the primary pharmacologic treatment for hyperglycemia when glycemic targets are not achieved with diet and exercise in gestational diabetes, and for all pregestational type 1 and type 2 diabetes in pregnancy (1A).

## Indications in Pregnancy

- Pregestational diabetes (type 1 or type 2)
- Gestational diabetes not controlled with diet ± metformin
- Fasting glucose > 95 mg/dL or 1-hour postprandial > 140 mg/dL or 2-hour > 120 mg/dL despite lifestyle

## Types Used in Pregnancy

- **Basal:** NPH insulin, insulin detemir, insulin glargine
- **Prandial:** Regular insulin, insulin lispro, insulin aspart
- **Continuous subcutaneous infusion (pump):** Increasingly used with CGM for type 1
