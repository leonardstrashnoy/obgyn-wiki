## Clinical Definition

Mifepristone (RU-486) is a progesterone receptor antagonist used for medical abortion, cervical priming, and Cushing syndrome treatment. In obstetrics, it is combined with misoprostol for first-trimester medical abortion (1A).

## Indications

- Medical abortion up to 10 weeks gestation (1A)
- Second-trimester medical abortion / fetal demise induction
- Cervical priming before surgical abortion
- Cushing syndrome (non-obstetric)

## Dosing / Technique

- **Medical abortion:** 200 mg PO day 1; misoprostol 800 mcg buccal 24–48h later (1A)
- Success rate: ~95–98% up to 10 weeks

## Contraindications

- Chronic adrenal failure
- Long-term corticosteroid therapy
- Bleeding disorder / anticoagulation
- Ectopic pregnancy (must be excluded first)
- IUD in place (remove first)
