# ACOG Practice Bulletin 183: Postpartum Hemorrhage (2017)

**Organization:** ACOG  
**Year:** 2017  
**Source:** See raw guideline file for URL

## Summary

Standard pharmacologic and surgical management of PPH. First-line uterotonics and tranexamic acid within 3 hours of onset.

## Key Recommendations (evidence-graded)

- **oxytocin** treats **postpartum_hemorrhage** (Level 1A, ACOG PB 183)
- **misoprostol** treats **postpartum_hemorrhage** (Level 1A, ACOG PB 183)
- **carboprost** treats **postpartum_hemorrhage** (Level 1A, ACOG PB 183)
- **methylergonovine** treats **postpartum_hemorrhage** (Level 1A, ACOG PB 183)
- **tranexamic_acid** treats **postpartum_hemorrhage** (Level 1A, WOMAN trial / ACOG)
